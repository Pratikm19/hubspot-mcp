# Recipes

End-to-end RevOps workflows for HubSpot + Gemini CLI / Claude Code.

Each recipe is a sequence of prompts with a clear goal, expected output, and time to complete. Run them in order. Copy, paste, done.

---

## Available Recipes

| Recipe | Goal | Time |
|---|---|---|
| [Monday Pipeline Review](./monday-pipeline-review.md) | Full pipeline health check before your week starts | 10 min |
| [Pre-QBR Client Audit](./pre-qbr-audit.md) | Everything you need before a client quarterly review | 15 min |
| [Churn Early Warning Scan](./churn-early-warning.md) | Catch at-risk customers before they cancel | 10 min |
| [ICP Prospecting Run](./icp-prospecting.md) | Find your best-fit unworked leads in one pass | 10 min |
| [Revenue Leak Finder](./revenue-leak-finder.md) | Surface where pipeline is silently disappearing | 15 min |

---

*Each recipe maps to a Pipeline OS module — the full HubSpot-native automation layer for teams who want this running automatically.*
